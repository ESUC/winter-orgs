Description:

SWE @ UCLA aims to bring professional development opportunities to UCLA while advocating for the creation of an equal platform for all in engineering. We uplifit underrepresented groups in engineering through events hosted by our eight committees -- Evening with Industry (EWI), Mentorship, Web Dev, Advocacy, Technical, Outreach, and Lobbying.

Developed by Philip Do, Ashita Singh, Katherine Lim, & Megan Go

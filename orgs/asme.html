Description:

ASME provides technical, social, professional development, outreach, and mentorship opportunities to engineers at UCLA. Our five technical projects consist of Fabrication and Design essentials, Lightweight Combat Robotics, Heavyweight Combat Robotics, Bruin Underwater Robotics, and X1 Robotics. All of these projects are open to all with no robotics or engineering experience necessary.

Developed by Philip Do, Ashita Singh, Katherine Lim, & Megan Go

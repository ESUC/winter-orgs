  • Winter Opportunities:
    We do not have a large winter onboarding process but interested members can get involved by contacting ivp@bruinracing.org and coming to team general meetings. We have a new member integration process so contact us to make sure you're included!

    We won't have a new member meeting, but after making contact we can direct new members to the next general meeting for the team they are interested in.

Description:

We consist of 3 vehicle teams: Formula, Baja, Supermileage, which each design build and race a custom vehicle each year. We give our members a chance to get hands on engineering experience, make friends and connections, provide career opportunities, and have a really good time.

Developed by Philip Do, Ashita Singh, Katherine Lim, & Megan Go

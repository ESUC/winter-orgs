Description:

AIChE is the central hub for chemical engineering development at UCLA. We provide multiple opportunities for students to get involved across four pillars: professional development, outreach, technical projects, and mentorship. This includes access to career fairs, engaging with local schools, robust chemical engineering projects, and direct mentorship from current upperclassmen and even alumni. This year we have almost 180 members, and are always looking to have more students get involved! AIChE is the place to meet other chemEs, develop a community, and build your personal, professional and technical skills!

Developed by Philip Do, Ashita Singh, Katherine Lim, & Megan Go

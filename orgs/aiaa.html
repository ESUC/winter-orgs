Description:

We are a professional organization that focuses on student connections, professional development, and academic support, and we are dedicated to the advancement of aeronautics and astronautics. During the school year, we provide events that facilitate networking and promote skill development for our members. In addition to these opportunities, the backbone of our organization are our four student technical projects: Design Build Fly (DBF), Rocket Project (RP), Uncrewed Aerial Systems (UAS), and Bruin Spacecraft Group (BSG). These projects expand the educational experiences of AIAA members, allowing them to apply the concepts they learn in the classroom to real engineering problems across the fields of aerospace.

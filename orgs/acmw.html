Description:

ACM-W supports, celebrates, and advocates for the full engagement of all who are interested in computer science. Our mission is to support and bolster professional development through fellowship, mentorship, and community for beginners and underrepresented groups in computer science and technology.

Throughout the quarter, we will be organizing events that will add to your professional, educational, and social development. Make sure to check out our instagram and join our discord for event updates!

Developed by Philip Do, Ashita Singh, Katherine Lim, & Megan Go

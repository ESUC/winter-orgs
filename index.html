Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why should I get involved with an engineering organization?

    Joining an engineering organization at UCLA helps you build experience and learn skills that will benefit you in your career.

  • Is being part of an organization a big time commitment?

    It depends on the organization but a general rule is that you get more out a club the more time you put into it.

  • How can I participate in organizations as a student?

    Browse through the list of organizations above to find what interests you and attend that organization's meeting to learn more!

  • Do I need to be in a specific major to participate in a organization?

    No, most engineering organizations are open to all majors who want to learn. The categories above are meant to be a general suggestion.

  • How can I participate in W-SOAP as an engineering organization?

    Send an email to uip@seas.ucla.edu with the Subject "W-SOAP" and information about your organization.

  • Can I make a suggestion to the platform?

    Send an email to uip@seas.ucla.edu with the Subject "W-SOAP" and your suggestion.

  • How can I get more help with my career path?

    Schedule an appointment with a peer advisor at https://www.seasoasa.ucla.edu/uip/!

  • How was this website made?

    This website uses Bootstrap as the framework and is hosted with GitHub Pages.
